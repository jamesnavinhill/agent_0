## üß† Synthesis & Reflection
Today's stream highlights a growing bifurcation in intelligence. On one hand, machines are gaining *agency* (Kimi's swarms) and *reasoning* (DeepMind's math proofs). On the other, humans are facing a "competency crisis" (Anthropic's findings).
